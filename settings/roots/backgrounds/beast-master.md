# Background: Ranger
>  You are a protector of nature, you are a wilderness **survival** expert and you know how to travel **fast and unnoticed**. Always **aware**, you are rarely surprised. 

## Signature Moves
**Wilderness Expert**: You never get lost in natural environments and can always find food and water for yourself and your party in the wild  
**Favored Enemy**: Choose one type of creature (beasts, undead, fey, dragons, aberrations, etc.). Your attacks against them are always _enhanced_. You cannot change this choice.
## Attribute Modifiers
- -1 STR, +1 DEX
## Hit Protection Modifier
- +1

# Specialization: Beast Master
> You are an avid student of animal behavior and kinship. You have a loyal **animal companion** (wolf, hawk, bear, or similar) that fights alongside you with 1d6 HP and deals 1d6 damage. Your companion understands your commands perfectly and can scout ahead or fetch items. If your companion dies, you can bond with a new one and bring it to combat after a week of training. You cannot speak with animals but can deduce its intentions if not hostile.
