# Background: Rogue
> You are the master of stealth, acrobatics and deception.

## Signature Move
**Backstab**: When you attack an unaware enemy from behind or from hiding, your attack is _enhanced_.
## Attribute Modifiers
- -1 STR, +1 DEX
## Hit Protection Modifier
- -1

# Specialization: Charlatan
> Your words are weapons and your disguises are armor. You can convincingly **impersonate others** if you've studied them. When you lie or manipulate, targets must make a **WIL save to doubt you**. You are an avid student of deception and your lie detection skills are _enhanced_. You start with a collection of disguises and forged documents.
