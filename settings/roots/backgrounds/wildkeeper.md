# Background: Druid
> You belong to the wild lands and feel uncomfortable in cities. You have faith in the elements and legendary beasts of old. You can **communicate with animals, plants and fungi** and always **find resources in the wilderness**. 

## Signature Move
**Primal Resilience**: You have +1 Armor when in natural environments (forests, swamps, wilderness)
**Natural Healing**: Once per day you can heal 1d4 STR as an action.
## Attribute Modifiers
- -1 DEX, +1 WIL
