# Background: Rogue
> You are the master of stealth, acrobatics and deception.

## Signature Move
**Backstab**: When you attack an unaware enemy from behind or from hiding, your attack is _enhanced_.
## Attribute Modifiers
- -1 STR, +1 DEX
## Hit Protection Modifier
- -1

# Specialization: Scout
>  You move like a shadow and see like a hawk. You can **move silently** even in difficult terrain and are **never surprised in wilderness** ambients. When you take the time to **observe an area**, the **Warden** must tell you the number and general location of nearby creatures. Your ranged attacks from hiding are _enhanced_.
