# Background: Ranger
>  You are a protector of nature, you are a wilderness **survival** expert and you know how to travel **fast and unnoticed**. Always **aware**, you are rarely surprised. 

## Signature Moves
**Wilderness Expert**: You never get lost in natural environments and can always find food and water for yourself and your party in the wild  
**Favored Enemy**: Choose one type of creature (beasts, undead, fey, dragons, aberrations, etc.). Your attacks against them are always _enhanced_. You cannot change this choice.
## Attribute Modifiers
- -1 STR, +1 DEX
## Hit Protection Modifier
- +1

# Specialization: Guardian
> You are an avid student of territorial protection and natural balance. Choose a **terrain type** (forest, mountain, swamp, etc.) as **your domain**. In your domain, enemies have _impaired_ stealth, and your attacks are _enhanced_. Creatures native to your domain will not attack you unless provoked.

- Rations (3 uses)
- Torches (3 uses)
- Expeditionary Gear
- a **Tool** of your choice
- Bandages (3 uses)
- Gambeson Armor (**+1 Armor**)
- Bow (d6 damage, _bulky_)
