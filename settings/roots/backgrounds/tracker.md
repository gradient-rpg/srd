# Background: Ranger
>  You are a protector of nature, you are a wilderness **survival** expert and you know how to travel **fast and unnoticed**. Always **aware**, you are rarely surprised. 

## Signature Moves
**Wilderness Expert**: You never get lost in natural environments and can always find food and water for yourself and your party in the wild  
**Favored Enemy**: Choose one type of creature (beasts, undead, fey, dragons, aberrations, etc.). Your attacks against them are always _enhanced_. You cannot change this choice.
## Attribute Modifiers
- -1 STR, +1 DEX
## Hit Protection Modifier
- +1

# Specialization: Tracker
> You are an avid student of signs and trails. You can **track** any creature through **any terrain**. You never lose a trail _unless magic obscures it_. When you study tracks, you can ask the **Warden** what do you learn about the creature's size, weight, how long ago they passed, and their general condition. In **wilderness environments**, you always **act first in combat**. When you take time to study an enemy's patterns, your next attack against them is _enhanced_.
