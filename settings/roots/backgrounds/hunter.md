# Background: Ranger
>  You are a protector of nature, you are a wilderness **survival** expert and you know how to travel **fast and unnoticed**. Always **aware**, you are rarely surprised. 

## Signature Moves
**Wilderness Expert**: You never get lost in natural environments and can always find food and water for yourself and your party in the wild  
**Favored Enemy**: Choose one type of creature (beasts, undead, fey, dragons, aberrations, etc.). Your attacks against them are always _enhanced_. You cannot change this choice.
## Attribute Modifiers
- -1 STR, +1 DEX
## Hit Protection Modifier
- +1

# Specialization: Hunter
> You are an avid student of legendary creatures, beasts or monsters and how to kill them, for coin of for pride. When you face a creature for the first time, you can ask the **Warden** one question about its abilities or weaknesses. Your attacks against beasts are _enhanced_. After 2 rounds of combat, you have studied a creature's attack pattern enough to make their attacks against you _impaired_ and you gain the reaction to immediately **counter-attack**. You may carry specialized repellents for different prey (silver powder, blessed oil, wolfsbane, etc.) and can take an action to imbue your weapon with them to gain _enchanced_ attacks against them.
