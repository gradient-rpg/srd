# Void Lord

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

_A monstrous, translucent mass that seems to drink at reality itself. Items it touches lose their edge and time runs thin nearby._

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 16 | 1 | 16 | 6 | 14 | engulf (d12), corrosive pseudopod (d10, _blast_)

## Traits

> **Reality Rend**: Can temporarily erase non-magical items (roll d6; on 1-2 an item is lost) and weaken magic in an area.

> **Phase**: Once per encounter can shift partially into the void, avoiding one physical attack.
