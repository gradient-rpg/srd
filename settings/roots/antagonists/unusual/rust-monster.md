# Rust Monster

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Armadillo-like creatures with club-like tails and long antennae. Feed on iron and rusted metal._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 5 | 16 | 13 | 5 | bite (d6) |

## Traits

> Any mundane metals their antennae touch instantly become rust. Can smell metal from a long distance away.

> **Critical Damage**: Metal armor rusts into useless weight.
