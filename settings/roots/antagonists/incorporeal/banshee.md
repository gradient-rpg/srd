# Banshee

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Incorporeal spirits that linger long after death to haunt the living._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 8 | 6 | 12 | 15 | ghostly touch (d8) |

## Traits

> Unharmed by cold, heat, or the elements. Iron weapon damage is _enhanced_.

> **Wail**: Anyone in earshot must make a WIL save or fall unconscious.
