# Wisp

#### **Rank 3** (_Swarm_, _Mob_)

_A ghostly mote of cold light, barely tethered to this plane._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | ------ |
| 1 | 6 | 11 | 10 | _chill touch (d4)_ |

## Traits

> Incorporeal Wisps pass through matter and unsettle the living with spectral cold.

> They are persistent nuisances and gather near sites of loss or violence.
