# Shadow

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

_Incorporeal monsters that look like animated shadows. Unharmed by mundane attacks, sleep or mind control._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 14 | 1 | 18 | 14 | draining touch (d6, ignores armor) |

## Traits

> **Critical Damage**: Target loses d4 STR. If reduced to 0 STR, they become a shadow as well.
