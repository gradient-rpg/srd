# Warp Panther

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Grotesque panther-like predators with long razored tentacles growing out of their backs._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 8 | 13 | 16 | 12 | tentacles (d8+d8, _blast_), bite (d10) |

## Traits

> Resistant to magical attacks. Caster must make a WIL save for the intended effect to succeed.

> Can teleport a short distance away at will. Hate Blink Dogs.
