# Fire Elemental

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

An inferno given purpose, the Fire Elemental burns with a hunger for flame. It consumes and ignites, leaving ash in its wake.

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 16 | 1 | 16 | 14 | 12 | engulfing flame (d12), fireburst (d10, _blast_) |

## Traits

> **Living Flame**: Immune to fire damage and ignites flammable materials on contact.

> **Flame Wave**: Once per encounter can unleash a cone of fire; targets must save DEX or suffer ongoing burn damage.

> **Critical Damage**: Victim suffers d6 WIL fire shock and continues to burn for d4 rounds, draining 1 HP each unless addressed.
