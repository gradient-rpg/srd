# Earth Elemental

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

A hulking heap of stone, soil, and iron, the Earth Elemental moves like an avalanche. It crushes and buries foes beneath its weight.

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 20 | 3 | 18 | 6 | 10 | quake (d12, _blast_), rock slam (d10) |

## Traits

> **Immovable**: Gains +2 to saving throws against forced movement and is resistant to bludgeoning damage.

> **Seismic Stomp**: Once per encounter can create a small tremor that stuns nearby enemies on a failed DEX save.

> **Critical Damage**: Victim is buried or crushed, taking d6 STR each round until freed.
