# Wind Elemental

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

Ephemeral currents given form, wind elementals are swift and relentless. They ride storms and scatter foes with howling gusts.

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 14 | 1 | 15 | 18 | 6 | gale (d10, _blast_), slicing gust (d8) |

## Traits

> **Airstride**: Immune to difficult terrain and can hover; melee attacks have a -2 penalty when the Wind Elemental is airborne.

> **Gust**: Once per encounter can push enemies back 10 feet and knock prone on a failed STR save.

> **Critical Damage**: Victim loses d4 DEX as their limbs are battered by relentless winds.
