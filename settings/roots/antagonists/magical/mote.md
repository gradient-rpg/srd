# Mote

#### **Rank 3** (_Swarm_, _Mob_)

_Tiny motes of pure elemental or arcane energy that flicker and collide._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | ------ |
| 1 | 6 | 9 | 10 | _spark (d4)_ |

## Traits

> Motes pulse with unstable power; when clustered they can disrupt spells and shock flesh.

> They are drawn to ley-lines, ritual sites, and erupting magic.
