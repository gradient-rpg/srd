# Crypt Guardian

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

_Animated skeletons clothed in billowing robes. Their eye sockets are hypnotically red. Defend crypts and tombs, but will not attack if left undisturbed._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12 | 12 | 11 | 14 | ethereal claws (d8+d8) |

## Traits

> Non-magical attacks against them are _impaired_.

> Can teleport any target in eyesight to a random room nearby.
