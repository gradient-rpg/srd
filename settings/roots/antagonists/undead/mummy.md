# Mummy

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Undead humanoids wrapped in funerary cloth. Found in pyramids, ruins, temples, and tombs._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 6 | 12 | 8 | 6 | necrotic touch (d10) |

## Traits

> **Critical Damage**: Target is infected with mummy rot, and is unable to recover STR until they are cured by a specialist healer. The target is also _deprived_.
