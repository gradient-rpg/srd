# Skeleton

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Frequently found serving a master or necromancer, often in groups of 3 or more._

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 5 | 1 | 8 | 13 | 0 | rusty sword (d6) or bow (d6) |

## Traits

> Found wearing whatever was on them at the time of their death.

> If a skeleton is killed and its bones are not scattered, it reforms.
