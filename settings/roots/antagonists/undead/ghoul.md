# Ghoul

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Grey-skinned man-things wearing shredded clothes that crave only the flesh of the living._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 6 | 14 | 8 | 3 | elongated claws (d6+d6) |

## Traits

> **Critical Damage**: Target is _deprived_ from their necrotic wound, dying in d4 days unless treated by a priest or skilled healer. If the body is not burned it later rises as a Ghoul as well.
