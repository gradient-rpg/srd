# Wisp

#### **Rank 3** (_Swarm_, _Mob_)

_A pale, drifting wisp of undeath, cold and hungry for warmth._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | ------ |
| 1 | 6 | 10 | 10 | _cold touch (d4)_ |

## Traits

> Undead Wisps chill the blood and sap the courage of those they haunt.

> They congregate in graveyards, crypts, and places of violent death.
