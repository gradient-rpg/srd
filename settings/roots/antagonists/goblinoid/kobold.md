# Kobold

#### **Rank 3** (_Swarm_, _Mob_)

_Small, reptilian humanoids with a knack for traps and pack tactics._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | ------ |
| 1 | 8 | 10 | 7 | _dagger (d4)_ |

## Traits

> Kobolds are cunning in numbers, using ambushes and improvised weapons.

> Individually weak, they compensate with traps and coordinated strikes.
