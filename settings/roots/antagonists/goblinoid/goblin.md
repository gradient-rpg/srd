# Goblin

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Small, grotesque humanoids with skin in earthly tones of green, brown, and grey._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 4 | 8 | 12 | 8 | dagger (d6) or sling (d6) |

## Traits

> Avoid combat, only attacking when advantageous, using hit-and-run tactics.

> Sometimes are found using dire wolves as mounts.
