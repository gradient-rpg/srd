# Troll

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

_Giant, warty humanoids of flesh and bark. Dwell underground or in deep forests, as well as the ruined homes of their former victims. Carnivorous._

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 14 | 1 | 14 | 12 | 4 | bite (d10), claws (d8+d8) |

## Traits

> When taking **Critical Damage**, a Troll will continue fighting, despite loss of flesh and limb. Only when their STR is 0 are they truly killed. Unless their heads are bathed in acid, fire, or powerful magic, they will fully regenerate within 1d4 days.
