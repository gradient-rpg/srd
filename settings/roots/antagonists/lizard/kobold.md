# Kobold

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Hairless humanoids with canine-like features and scaly skin. Can see perfectly in the dark, but are harmed by direct sunlight._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |

| 3 | 8 | 13 | 4 | bite (d6), spear (d8) |

## Traits

> Use traps and ambush to defeat opponents. Often act in service of a more powerful creatures.
