# Hydra

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

_Large reptilians with nine serpentine heads. Very difficult to throw off once their quarry is detected._

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12 | 2 | 13 | 7 | 12 | bite (d12, _blast_), _detachment_ |

## Traits

> On taking **Critical Damage**, the hydra loses one head. It then continues fighting until no heads remains.
