# Naga

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Humanoids with the lower body, tail, and neck of snakes. Dwell in hot jungles and temples lost to time._

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 6 | 1 | 14 | 12 | 14 | sword (d6), bite (d10) |

## Traits

> Use cunning and hypnosis to capture other creatures to be enslaved.

> **Gaze**: A target facing the Naga is hypnotized, falling under its will. Its victims cannot be compelled to violence.
