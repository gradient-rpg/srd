# Druid

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

_Venerable keepers of wild magic and ancient groves, Druids bind the elements and the beasts to their will. They stand between civilization and the wilder things of the world, enforcing nature's ancient laws._

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 14 | 1 | 8 | 13 | 16 | thorn spear (d10), nature blast (d8, _blast_) |

## Traits

> Guardians of sacred places; can call storms, entangle foes, and assume animal forms.

> Can cast the following spells at will: _Entangle_, _Call Beast_, _Heal_.
