# Sea Hag

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Ghostly, green-skinned hags that dwell in seaweed forests and warm shallow waters._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 6 | 11 | 15 | 14 | long claws (d6+d6) |

## Traits

> Immune to magic from spellbooks.

> **Hideous Gaze**: A target is overcome with fear (HP drops to 0). WIL save each round to break the spell.
