# Red Cap

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Born from blood left to rot in the land of Fairy. Their hats are a deep red from the blood they bathe in._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 6 | 6 | 12 | 8 | dual sickles (d6+d6) |

## Traits

> Ignore those with little to live for, as there is no pleasure in their murder.

> **Critical Damage**: Target is eviscerated, the spray of their blood restoring STR loss suffered by the Red Cap.
