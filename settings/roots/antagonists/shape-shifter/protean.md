# Protean

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

_An ancient master of forms, the Protean bends flesh and bone into any shape it desires. It walks among mortals under borrowed faces._

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 14 | 1 | 14 | 16 | 18 | morphic strike (d12, ignores armor), claw (d8)

## Traits

> **Shapechange**: Instantly assume any humanoid or beast form; gains one feature of the assumed form while transformed.

> **Mimicry**: Can perfectly imitate voices and mannerisms; creatures must save WIL to detect illusions.
