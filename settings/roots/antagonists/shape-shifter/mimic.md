# Mimic

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Malicious shape shifters that take on the form of inanimate objects commonly found in dungeons._

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 9 | 2 | 13 | 6 | 12 | bite (d8) |

## Traits

> Remain motionless while in disguise, surprising anyone that comes near.

> The mimic's saliva is extremely sticky and difficult to remove. Alchemists will pay up to 150gp for even the smallest vial.
