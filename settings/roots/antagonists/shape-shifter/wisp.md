# Wisp

#### **Rank 3** (_Swarm_, _Mob_)

_A flickering, mutable spirit that mimics simple shapes before dissolving._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | ------ |
| 1 | 6 | 11 | 9 | _twist (d4)_ |

## Traits

> Shape-shifter Wisps take on minor forms to distract or harass foes, rarely holding a single guise.

> They prefer confusion over direct damage, making them nuisances in groups.
