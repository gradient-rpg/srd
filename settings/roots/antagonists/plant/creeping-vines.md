# Creeping Vines

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Appear as an inert, typical vine. Attacks passing creatures to use their corpses as fertilizer._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 8 | 10 | 12 | 2 | vines (d4, _blast_) |

## Traits

> **Critical Damage**: Target is pulled into the air where they asphyxiate within a few minutes.
