# Sentient Tree

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

_A living mountain of bark, root, and living soil. Where it strides, saplings scramble and the ground cracks underfoot._

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 18 | 3 | 18 | 3 | 14 | root crush (d12), entangling sweep (d10, _blast_)

## Traits

> **Animate Grove**: Can animate nearby trees and plants as allies (use Treant stats) once per encounter.

> **Regrowth**: Regains 4 HP at the start of its turn unless burned or completely severed from earth.
