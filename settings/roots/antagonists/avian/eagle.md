# Eagle

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

> Large, nimble raptors that patrol cliffs and river valleys; prized by hunters for their precise strikes.

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 3 | 12 | 14 | 11 | talons (d8), beak (d6, _pierce_) |

## Traits

> **Keen Sight**: An Eagle automatically succeeds on one WIL check per encounter to spot distant or hidden prey.

> **Stooping Strike**: When performing a dive attack from above, an Eagle deals an extra d6 damage.

> **Territorial**: Eagles are aggressive toward intruders near nests and gain advantage on the first attack of the encounter.
