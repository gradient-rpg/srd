# Falcon

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

> Swift hunters of open plains and high ledges, Falcons rely on speed and precision to overwhelm prey.

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 4 | 13 | 15 | 11 | talons (d8+d6), dive (d10, _pierce_) |

## Traits

> **Aerial Agility**: Falcons can make rapid reposition moves in combat, allowing them to avoid opportunity attacks when moving away from a target.

> **Precision Strike**: Once per encounter a Falcon may add +2 damage to a successful talons attack representing a perfectly aimed strike.

> **Focused Hunter**: Falcons receive a bonus to tracking rolls when pursuing a single target.
