# Gizmo

#### **Rank 3** (_Swarm_, _Mob_)

_A small, clattering construct cobbled from spare parts and clockwork._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | ------ |
| 1 | 7 | 10 | 6 | _clack (d4)_ |

## Traits

> Mechanical and erratic, Gizmos move in curious jerks and often appear in groups.

> Their components are fragile but numerous, making them more dangerous en masse.
