# Gargoyle

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Magical, slow-moving statues of monstrous winged creatures, typically employed as guards or watchdogs._

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 8 | 3 | 14 | 4 | 12 | claws (d8+d8) |

## Traits

> Prowl at nights but fear the day, when the sun's light freezes them in place.

> Immune to charms and magical sleep.
