# Blood Elk

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Born from violence resulting from a greedy or selfish act._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 4 | 12 | 13 | 5 | horns (d8) |

## Traits

> Kill their victims for sustenance, but gains no succor. They are always hungry.

> **Critical Damage**: Gores its victims by ripping out their entrails.
