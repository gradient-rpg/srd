# Dire Boar

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

_Gnashing, tusked brutes from deep wilds that charge with bone-crushing force. Dire Boars can be as big as bears, root through undergrowth and smash anything that blocks their way._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 13 | 16 | 12 | 11 | gore (d8+d6), trampling charge (d8, _knockback_) |

## Traits

> **Rampaging Charge**: When a Dire Boar moves at least 6m before attacking, its attack is _enhanced_.

> **Tough Hide**: Reduces incoming slashing damage by 1.
