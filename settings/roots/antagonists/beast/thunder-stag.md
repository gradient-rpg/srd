# Thunder Stag

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

>A towering stag whose hooves strike like rolling thunder. Thunder Stags roam high plateaus, stamping the earth to unnerve predators and herd their kin.

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12 | 17 | 13 | 12 | antler sweep (d10), thunder-stomp (d10, _stagger_) |

## Traits

> **Stormstep**: When the Thunder Stag uses thunder-stomp it can force grounded foes to make a STR save or be staggered for their next action.

> **Antler Hold**: On a successful antler sweep dealing a critical hit, the stag may pin a Small target, preventing movement until freed.
