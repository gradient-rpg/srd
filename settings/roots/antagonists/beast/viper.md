# Viper

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Poisonous snakes often found in caves, pits, and other dark places. An antitoxin can be made from their poison sacs._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 3 | 5 | 12 | 3 | bite (d6) |

## Traits

> Use heat and smell to track their victims.

> **Critical Damage**: Target is poisoned, dying in 1d12 hours without a cure.
