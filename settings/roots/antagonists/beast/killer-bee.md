# Killer Bees

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Oversized bees that build underground hives. Hyper-aggressive, attack anything that comes near._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 6 | 6 | 14 | 8 | sting (d6), _detachment_ |

## Traits

> Produce special honey that heals 1d6 STR. 3 uses.

> When the bees cause STR damage, the stingers are lodged into the target, dealing d4 damage each round until removed.
