# Mind Lasher

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

_Humanoid creatures with purple skin and squid-like heads. Dwell deep underground or in spacefaring vessels. Feed on the brains of other humanoids, absorbing their memories and life essence. Highly intelligent, but evil beyond belief._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 12 | 8 | 12 | 18 | tentacles (d6, _blast_) |

## Traits

> **Mind Blast**: Nearby targets must save WIL or lose 1d4 WIL and become paralyzed.

> **Critical Damage**: A tentacle rips the victim's brain out of their skull.
