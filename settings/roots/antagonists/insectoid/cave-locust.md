# Cave Locust

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Giant plant-eating crickets that dwell in caves. Immune to most types of poison._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 2 | 6 | 12 | 3 | bite (d6) |

## Traits

> Their natural coloration allows them to blend in with the stone. They emit loud shrieks when threatened.
