# Broodmother

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

_A hulking matron of a vast, subterranean hive. Her body swells with eggs and her maw drips corrosive ichor._

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 18 | 2 | 18 | 8 | 12 | crushing jaws (d12), brood spurt (d8, _blast_), _detachment_

## Traits

> **Spawn**: Once per encounter can spawn a small detachment of larvae (use Cave Locust stats) that act on her turn.

> **Acidic Blood**: When wounded, blood sprays in a 5-foot cone dealing d6 acid damage; metal corrodes quickly.
