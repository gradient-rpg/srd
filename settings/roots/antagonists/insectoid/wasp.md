# Wasp

#### **Rank 3** (_Swarm_, _Mob_)

_A small, aggressive stinging insect that attacks in numbers._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | ------ |
| 1 | 6 | 11 | 7 | _sting (d4)_ |

## Traits

> Wasps strike quickly and retreat, their painful stings distracting foes.

> They form nests and will defend them tenaciously in swarms.
