# Burrowing Horror

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Huge insectoids with multiple legs and shiny black eyes. Subsist from dirt, roots, and flesh._

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 6 | 1 | 16 | 11 | 4 | bite (d10), acid squirt (d8, _blast_) |

## Traits

> Burrow just beneath surfaces to ambush unsuspecting creatures.

> **Critical Damage**: Target loses a body part. Roll 1d6. 1: Head, 2-4: Leg, 5-6: Arm.
