# Storm Giant

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

_Building-sized humanoids with bronze-hued skin and light-colored hair. Live on the highest mountain peaks and rockiest coastlines._

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 18 | 2 | 18 | 16 | 18 | great sword (d12), _detachment_ |

## Traits

> Unharmed by lightning, can summon storm clouds at their will. Can fly with the wind as a their chariot.

> **Thunderclap**: Deals 4 STR damage against a single target. Targets wearing metal armor take double damage.
