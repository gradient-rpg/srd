# Cutpurse

#### **Rank 3** (_Swarm_, _Mob_)

_A nimble thief who picks pockets and moves through crowds unnoticed._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | ------ |
| 1 | 7 | 11 | 8 | _slash (d4)_ |

## Traits

> Quick-fingered and slippery, Cutpurses prefer hit-and-run thefts.

> They may attempt to steal rather than fight, avoiding prolonged engagements.
