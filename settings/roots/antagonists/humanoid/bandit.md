# Bandit

#### **Rank 2** (_Stalker_, _Vanguard_, _Tactician_)

_Outlaws who raid settlements, attack ships, or ambush travelers. Groups of four or more are a _detachment_._

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 4 | 1 | 12 | 12 | 9 | short sword (d6) or short bow (d6) |

## Traits

> A detachment always travels with one leader wearing chain mail or similar (2 Armor) and a long sword (d10) or crossbow (d8).

> **Loyal**: When testing Morale, save using the leader's WIL (13). If the leader dies, the others will flee.
