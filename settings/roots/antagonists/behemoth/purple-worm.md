# Purple Worm

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

_Gargantuan worms that lair in enormous burrows deep underground, only coming to the surface to eat. Found in high deserts and hot tundra._

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 18 | 1 | 18 | 8 | 6 | bite (d12), _detachment_ |

## Traits

> **Critical Damage**: The target is devoured, taking d8 STR damage from the acid in the worm's stomach.
