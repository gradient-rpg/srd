# Dark Commander

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

_A proud and ruthless warlord who rose to command legions of monsters. Once a noble conqueror, he embraced forbidden power and now combines brutal martial might with dark sorcery to crush kingdoms and bend lesser beings to his will._

| HP | Armor | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 16 | 2 | 16 | 12 | 18 | soul bite (d12), terror howl (d8, _blast_) |

## Traits

> **Aura of Night**: Creatures within 10 feet suffer -2 to DEX while the Dark Commander is present.

> **Dreamwalk**: Can step into a sleeping target's dreams to inflict WIL damage or extract memories.
