# Wisp

#### **Rank 3** (_Swarm_, _Mob_)

_A faint, flickering mote of shadow and light, haunted by an otherworldly presence._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | ------ |
| 1 | 6 | 11 | 9 | _nudge (d4)_ |

## Traits

> Elusive and insubstantial, Wisps drift through the dark and confuse foes.

> They rarely act alone, preferring to swarm and harry enemies with minor touches.
