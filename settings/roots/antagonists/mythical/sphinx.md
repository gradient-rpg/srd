# Sphinx

#### **Rank 1** (_Elite_, _Juggernaut_, _Controller_)

_Mythical beasts with the upper body and head of a human, the wings of an eagle, and the body of a lion. Immune to magic of any kind._

| HP | STR | DEX | WIL | Attack |
| -- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 18 | 12 | 13 | 18 | claws (d8+d8, _blast_), beak (d10) |

## Traits

> Poses riddles to anyone they meet. Failure to answer correctly is usually met with death. They know almost everything worth knowing.

> **Roar**: Anyone in earshot must save WIL or flee in fear.
